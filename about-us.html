About - via NARA

About NARA

Introducing Dr. Nasrin Rabiei Karahroudi, the visionary founder of via NARA. With a diverse background spanning from Tehran to Kuala Lumpur, Nara brings a unique blend of cultural richness and contemporary artistry to her interior design work. As a contemporary artist and educator, she approaches design with a holistic perspective, emphasizing the importance of knowledge, history, and theory in creating meaningful spaces.

Creating Spaces with via NARA

At the heart of Nara’s approach is a dedication to creating spaces that not only look exceptional but also enhance the quality of life for those who inhabit them. She believes in the power of thoughtful design to evoke emotions, promote well-being, and reflect the personality and aspirations of the occupants.

Recognizing that each project is a collaboration between Nara and the client, she places great emphasis on understanding individual preferences, lifestyle, and functional needs. This collaborative approach ensures that the result is not just aesthetically pleasing but also tailored to the client’s unique identity.

Always at the forefront of design innovation, Nara embraces new ideas, materials, and technologies to create spaces that are not only visually stunning but also ahead of the curve. She continuously seeks inspiration from diverse sources, ensuring a fresh and dynamic approach to each project.

A global contemporary creative at heart, Dr Nasrin Rabiei Karahroudi (NARA) set sail to Malaysia back in 2008 upon graduating from the University of Tehran, majoring in Art and Architecture. In Malaysia, her research in design led to a Phd Doctorate in Design, engaging in countless collaborations with leading international artists, designers and movements alike.

NARA has over the years garnered recognition and numerous acclaims amongst educationist and creatives alike. Her works are regularly exhibited in expos in Malaysia, Iran and Australia, while her lectures reach as far as Copenhagen, Denmark.

Her rich experience has culminated with the founding of ViaNARA, based in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. This design practice serves as a styling house that strives to set the benchmark for regional contemporary interior design.
