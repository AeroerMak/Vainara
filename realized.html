Realised Projects - via NARA

Design with Purpose

Rooms are more than just the absence of space or the addition of decor, but we believe they should be expressions of the heart. At our multiple locations, our mission remains synonymous to the intricacies of art and lifestyle: to create truly custom design experiences via the exploration, creation and visualization of unique furniture pieces.

The environments we design and build should not follow fads, trends, styles or seek only the dramatic and spectacular gesture. On the contrary, they must be living, welcoming, empathic, flexible, full of different possibilities of interaction that can, over time, transform the lives of the individuals who occupy them.

We differ because we incorporate all of our visions within each curated piece of furniture. Because everyone can sell you a sofa or console, but we’re here to tell your story.
